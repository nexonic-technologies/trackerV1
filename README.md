# Tracker V1

> A modular, service-driven ERP and HRMS platform designed to streamline workforce management and business operations.

## Overview

TrackerV1 is an integrated Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) and Human Resource Management System (HRMS) built with a modular and extensible architecture. The platform centralizes business processes, automates operational workflows, and provides a unified system for managing employees, attendance, payroll, recruitment, onboarding, and organizational data.

The system is designed with scalability, maintainability, and enterprise-grade security in mind, making it suitable for growing organizations that require a flexible and customizable business management platform.

## Architecture Principles

TrackerV1 follows several core principles:

- **Modularity** – Business modules remain loosely coupled and independently extensible.
- **Scalability** – Designed to support future modules without architectural rewrites.
- **Security** – Policy-driven authorization and controlled data access.
- **Maintainability** – Generic frameworks minimize duplication and simplify enhancements.
- **Business First** – Technology decisions are driven by operational requirements.
